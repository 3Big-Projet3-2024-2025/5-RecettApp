Description: A plant-based recipe full of flavors.

Recipe Type: Vegan

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients
  • Tofu - 300 grams
  • Vegetables - 2 cups
  • Soy Sauce - 2 tbsp
Instructions

Stir fry tofu and vegetables, add soy sauce and serve.
